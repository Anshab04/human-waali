🚨 Observation #1: Brand Consistency Lapses

A few ads use different fonts or image filters than the ones on your homepage. This may sound small — but it hits trust subconsciously.

🧠 Observation #2: Generic Copy is Holding Back Great Ideas

Instead of “Grow faster with AI,” what if we said: “How 3 Startups Used Clevertize AI to Triple ROI in 60 Days”?

🧭 Observation #3: Broken Funnel Experience

Clicked an ad → landed on homepage → forgot why I came. Let’s make CTAs go directly to the value (and let’s track them like crazy).
